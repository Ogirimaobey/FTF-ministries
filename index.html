The Prophetic Word for 2025

GLORY TO GOD!!

An end has come to pain and struggles for we have broken through into a new season with honor, those who know us will need to look again for we have broken through with honor. The old is gone, the new has come. We just need to rejoice for we are now growing and glowing and there's nothing the enemies can do.

Those who resist our rising this Time shall perish, those who oppose it go down without Mercy. We have passed the test, we have crossed over. Glory to God. We are full of Beauty and glory.

We are in our shining Armor and we have taken over and this beauty is sealed by the blood sacrifice of Jesus. It's our month of victory, it's our time of Glory, no turning back, no losing, we are in charge and we are reigning in life.

Glory to God forever more. Give the Lord a shout of Praise and Glory.
